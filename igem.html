The Problem

Unknowingly taking drugs which are contaminated with dangerous cutting agents or other more potent drugs kills thousands each year.

Accidental overdoses due to users not knowing the purity of the drugs they are taking also costing hundreds of lives.

Our Solution

We are creating a cell- free paper-based biosensor that can tell a user exactly what is in the drugs they are taking.

Our biosensor will detect for specific compounds in drug samples and give a semi-quantitative analysis of the purity of certain drugs.
