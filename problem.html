    Class-A-fiED aims to take drugs out of the shadows, to facilitate a conversation, and recognise that this is an important issue that needs addressing. We do not want drug users to stay "Classified" - hidden away, too afraid to seek help, we want to communicate an important message - stay safe and recover.
