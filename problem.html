Changing views

Class-A-fiED aims to take drugs out of the shadows, to facilitate a conversation, and recognise that this is an important issue that needs addressing. We do not want drug users to stay "Classified" - hidden away, too afraid to seek help, we want to communicate an important message - stay safe and recover.

Why our biosensor?

To support those who are not quite at the stage of making the transition to recovery. True figures can be hard to attain - hidden nature of the issue. But we have searched hard. The true figures may greatly exceed the published ones. Many slip under the radar. Drugs affect every aspect of society - health, medicine, crime, economics, education, social inequality, law, family life. In this project though we want to go down to the core of the issue.
